# Roasted Carrots



Ingredients
* Carrots (1 kilo)
* Thyme (several sprigs)
* Dried Oregano (1 TSP)
* Extra Virgin Olive Oil (enough to coat carrots)
* Salt (to taste)

Utensils
* All-Clad Stainless-Steel Pan with Lid


Directions
1. Cut carrots into long, skinny pieces (halve or quarter lengthwise then cut into thirds crosswise)
1. Mix all ingredients together and put into stainless steel pan
1. Roast with lid on at 300 for an hour.
1. Take lid off and roast at 400 with convection, stirring every 10 minutes, until desired crispiness
